ALLCAL

Helping you organize your events



Allcal is currently available as an iOS App, an Android app and as a web app. It can help you easily plan and organize social events. Click below to know more

App Features



Organize Events

Allcal lets you organize your events effortlessly.

Realtime RSVPs

Your users will be able to set their RSVP for individual events and you can see the user data in real-time.

Unlimited Events

There is no limit on the number of events that you are allowed to create.

Unlimited Users

No limit on the number of events and of course, no limit on the number of users.

Detailed Event Sharing

You can manually fine tune the sharing capabilities of your users.

Multiplatform

Allcal is available across iOS, Android and on the Web.

Product Services



Enter Event Schedule

Don't worry if you have a lot of events, we will take care of entering the event details into the Calendar.

Custom Schedule Page

We will create an easily integrateable webpage for registration and schedule that matches your event site.

Online Registration

Users will be able to register online and RSVP for events within the calendar.

Reports

Customer Service
