Allcal

A community-driven app for organizing your conference, members, and events



Allcal keeps your group connected anywhere with a real-time schedule, event reminders, and automatic updates. Available on iPhone, Android, iPad, or on your Desktop.

App Features



Stay Organized

Create a mobile schedule you can share instantly.

Automatic Updates

Send changes to everyone with one click.

Real-time Data

Keep track of members’ RSVPs and see who’s coming to your events.

Unlimited Events

Create an unlimited number of events for your group or conference.

Unlimited Users

Share your schedule with anyone - there’s no limit to how many people can join your calendar.

Multi-Platform Tool

Allcal is available to download on iPhone, Android, iPad, and your Desktop.

Product Services



Calendar and Event Management

We know you’re busy. Let us help by setting up your entire conference or group schedule for you with 10 days.

Website Integration

Sync everything instantly by integrating your conference or events schedule directly into your website.

Custom Calendar Display

Design a custom schedule to match your branding and blend seamlessly into your existing website.

In-Depth Reports

Allcal’s admin page lets you export detailed reports about your members, attendance, and trends throughout your conference or event.

Ticketing

COMING SOON: Sell tickets to events directly in the Allcal app.

Customer Service

Allcal team members are always on call to help make sure you and your attendees have a wonderful event experience. Want a little more? We can even send Allcal team members to your event as your on-site customer service team.
